Bridging Cinema and Markets

However, through our rigorous structural analysis, we have identified that both are driven by collective human psychology and established logical frameworks. Whether it is the 'Three-Act Structure' in a narrative or 'Session Liquidity Cycles' in a market, the underlying mechanics of progression and reversal remain consistent.
