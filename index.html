Project Overview

Uncoverlogic is a multi-disciplinary research initiative dedicated to identifying structural patterns in seemingly disparate fields. Our primary objective is to bridge the gap between human storytelling and market behavior—two domains governed by collective psychology and established logical frameworks.

By dissecting cinematic narratives and session-based market movements, we provide a unique perspective on how "structure" dictates outcome. This hub serves as a centralized gateway to our specialized databases and execution journals.

01 Cinematic Analysis: Decoding the structural logic of narratives and scripts.
02 Market Mechanics: Observing institutional order flow and session dynamics.
03 Execution Logs: Documenting the psychology of decision-making.
04 Data Archive: A permanent repository of structural research notes.
